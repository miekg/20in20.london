Thank you all!

We've raised a fantastic £10,442!

This will enable the MS Trust to continue its vital work supporting many of the 100,000 people in the UK with MS and can be used to help in a variety of ways:

  • · Cover the cost of providing their personalised enquiry service to all those living within the London Boroughs for six months.
  • · They would be able to produce, publish and post two editions of their quarterly newsletter ‘Open Door’ to all those people living with MS within the London Boroughs.
  • · 20 MS Nurses from the region could attend the MS Trust’s annual conference, which brings together over 300 multidisciplinary health and social care professionals who share a commitment to improve MS care.
